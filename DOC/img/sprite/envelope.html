My Profile A Little About Me

I’m a native Californian, youngest of 5, born Riverside California and raised in Mexicali, Mexico. I’m driven, independent, and self-sufficient. The first in my family to earn a college degree, I received a B.A. from Cal State San Marcos (CSUSM) in Visual and Performing Arts and Technology, and made Deans List. While at CSUSM, I studied Japanese language and then immersed myself in Japanese culture, specifically Sumi-e painting and Japanese cinema (1949-1987) during a semester at Kansai Gaidai University in Hirakata City, Japan.

Because I love to learn, and to stay current with the new economy, I recently completed a 3-month intensive Web Development boot camp at Coding Dojo in Mountain View California. I also go to Noisebrdige in San Francisco and took a course at SF State University to continue developing my skills as a Jr. Web Developer. I’m now pretty comfortable with Http Request/Response, HTML5, CSS3, Git/GitHub, Twitter Bootstrap, Responsive Design, jQuery, jQueryUI, Database Design, SQL Queries, Basic Algorithms, and Web Security (sql injection, cross site scripting).

I enjoy pair programming and led and managed a small team at Coding Dojo to create our graduation project. Because I also have a wealth of business experience to draw on, I'm not only a Jr. Web Developer - I'm a well-rounded professional.

In my spare time, I paint exploring surrealism while using Japanese painting techniques with modern acrylic media. I infuse my paintings with my Mexican cultural heritage. I'm also learning to play the violin and ukulele. I am also part of a pack that includes three wonderful dogs, Cacomixtle, Pepito Pokchuck, and Chico Poztoc.
